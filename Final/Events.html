Cycle the Continental Divide

Have a desire to test your cycling mettle? Try biking “Cycle the Continental Divide” or more commonly know now as the “Tour Divide” race. It begins June 7, 2018 in Banff, Alberta Canada and takes 40 days to traverse mountain forests, valleys, grasslands and deserts, ending in Antelope Wells, New Mexico. This trail consists of 2,754 miles of mud, sand, snow and just about any type of environment you can imagine! It will definitely test your abilities and is not for the faint at heart or beginning cyclist. There is no entry fee or early registration required.
