Need a Tune-Up??

We offer four different tune-ups options:

The basic tune-up includes brake inspection and adjustments, derailleur inspection and adjustments, tire inspection and inflation, safety check of all bolts and wipe down of frame.

The premium tune-up everything included in the Basic Tune-Up, plus wheel truing, pivot point lube, chain clean and lube and frame clean and polish.

The brake system tune-up includes brake pad installation, brake inspection and adjustments and inspection of cables and housing.

The drivetrain tune-up includes drive system inspection and adjustments, inspection of cables and housing and quick chain clean and lube.
